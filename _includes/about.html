About Me

Hello, Everyone.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in DYROS Lab (Prof. Jaeheung Park) at Seoul National University, Republic of Korea.

In 2013, my colleagues and I had participated in KOREA AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE CONTEST 2013 as Team Smart Mobility and I was student subleader of our team. My main roles were conducting vehicle control tests, lane detection using lidar sensors, and managing the entire team schedule.

In 2015, my colleagues and I had participated in DARPA Robotics Challenge Finals 2015 (DRC Finals 2015) as Team SNU My main role was to carry out the first mission of the challenge using a humanoid to control the steering of the vehicle to the parking lot without colliding with obstacles.

Main keywords of my reasearches are Intelligent Vehicle, Motion Planning and Path Following, Optimal Control, and Machine Learning for object recognition.
